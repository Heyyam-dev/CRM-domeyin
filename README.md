## 🇬🇧 English

### About the Project

**DOMEİN** is a startup focused on building professional websites for clients. Our goal is to provide powerful, fast, and modern web solutions to businesses of all sizes — from small entrepreneurs to large corporations.

We don't just build websites. We manage the entire lifecycle of your online identity — from design to ongoing support.

---

### Mission

> Bringing Azerbaijani businesses into the digital world — simply, accessibly, and effectively.

---

### What We Offer

| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Corporate Website** | Professional, multi-section sites for company presentation |
| **Landing Page** | High-conversion single-page sites for products or services |
| **Online Store** | E-commerce solutions — catalog, payment, order management |
| **Portfolio Site** | Sites showcasing your personal brand and work |
| **Technical Support** | Updating, optimizing, and securing existing websites |

---

### Target Audience

- Small and medium businesses seeking an online presence
- Professionals building a personal brand (doctors, lawyers, architects, etc.)
- Entrepreneurs looking to sell their products online

---

### Technology Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React / Next.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Python (Django/FastAPI)
- **CMS:** WordPress, Webflow (based on client needs)
- **Hosting:** Cloudflare, Vercel, VPS solutions
- **Design:** Figma-based UI/UX prototyping
